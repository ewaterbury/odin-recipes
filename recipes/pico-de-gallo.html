Pico de Gallo

Description

This simple salsa will serve as a bright, refreshing, and possibly authentic compliment to any mexican cuisine. This delicious and convienient recipie incorporates fresh tomato, onion, and jalapeño to create a perfect pairing for the finest taco, burito, or nacho plate. The best part is it only takes 15 minutes to prepare.

Check out the recipe below for the perfect way to up your taco-tuesday game!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Finely Chopped Roma Tomatoes
  • 1 Large, Finely Chopped White or Yellow Onion
  • Juice of 2 Limes
  • 2 Deseeded, Finley Chopped Jalapeños
  • 1/2 c. Finely Chopped Cilantro
  • 1 1/2 tsp. Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 tsp. Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Combine chopped tomatoes, onions, and jalapeños in a bowl with lime juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the cilantro and toss ingredients together until all ingredient are evenly mixed.
  3. Cover bowl and let salsa chill in the refrigerator for at least an hour. Serve with chips or use as a topping for tacos or buritos.
